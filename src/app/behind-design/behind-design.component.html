Behind the Design

LUXE CREATIVE - Founder + CEO "Martin Potoczny" is a pioneering Creative Director working in the worlds of luxury events, the arts, fashion, marketing + lifestyle design. Martin’s studio consists of nationally recognized designers, producers and artists who conjure and create distinctive, meaningful and culturally relevant work.
    
He partners with the top echelon of companies, cultural institutions and private clients alike. They share a common desire for innovative design and seamless production. His collaborators include: The Los Angeles Film School, Madonna, Roger Waters, The Pittsburgh Zoo & PPG Aquarium, Fortune 500 executives, Microsoft and Chanel. His studio’s vision is unprecedented, highly sought-after and has raised the bar for industry standards. Currently, the art + technology collaborative, is working on commissioned art installations, styling exquisite events and developing creative concepts for businesses worldwide.

Martin’s creations have seen the global stage, taking his work and vision around the world. Remaining loyal to his hometown, he continues to thrive while expressing his creativity in America’s “Most Livable City" and abroad. Martin is currently developing a creative incubator that will change the game of artist expression and mentorship. Martin’s commitment in helping to develop the city’s revival through his creative design and global perspective is unwavering.
